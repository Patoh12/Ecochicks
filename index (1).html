About Ecochick Kenya

Founded in 2018, Ecochick Kenya has grown to become a trusted name in poultry equipment supply across Kenya. Our mission is to empower poultry farmers with high-quality, affordable equipment that helps them maximize their productivity.

All our products are selected or designed specifically for Kenyan farming conditions, ensuring they perform reliably even in challenging environments.
